Mango Doge

Mango Doge is an ERC20 token bringing memes and mangos to the world of decentralized finance.

Much like the early days of the internet, we hope to usher in the adoption of Web3 and get more people using this new technology.

How to Buy
1. Create a MetaMask wallet by visiting this website to set up your Ethereum wallet in Google Chrome or on mobile.
2. Buy Ethereum (ETH) from an exchange like coinbase or binance and transfer it to your MetaMask wallet.
3. Go to Uniswap and connect your wallet. Paste in the $MDOGE address: 0xD93b88fBc8BC392523a94a086576c73bb8d305B9.
4. Enter the amount of ETH you would like to exchange for $MDOGE and click swap.
